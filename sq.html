SnappyQuiz is a web-based application that allows users to play short quizes to test and improve their vocabulary. The original concept for the game was intended for students who speak English as a second language in third-world countries. With this application, individuals will be able to make an account and connect it to a social network of their choice. This way scores can be compared and those who rise to the top in their vocabulary comprehension will shine.

The application is not yet completed, but current progress can be viewed at SnappyQuiz.com!
